Aftercare Agreement

I confirm that I have received and understood the following aftercare instructions, and that I am fully responsible for the care of my tattoo after leaving the studio. Tridatu Tattoo is not liable for complications resulting from improper aftercare.

  1. Use the second skin (repairing film derm) for at least 12 hours up to 3 days, peel it off and gently wash the tattoo area with lukewarm water, then just tap it with a paper towel or tissue to dry it. If you notice excessive fluid buildup, blistering, rash, or irritation under the second skin before the recommended time, remove it early and clean the area gently.
  2. Apply a THIN layer of the ointment (tattoo aftercare cream) recommended by the tattoo artist, 1–3 times a day, just to keep it a bit moist.
  3. Keep the tattoo clean and dry, avoid soaking the tattoo in water — showers are okay, baths are not. (Of course, no swimming wherever.)
  4. Avoid direct sunlight on the tattoo while it's healing.
  5. Wear loose, clean clothing over the tattooed area to avoid friction and allow air circulation. Avoid gym, sauna, hot yoga, or any activity causing excessive sweating and friction for at least 1–2 weeks.
  6. Be patient with the healing progress — it takes time, and everyone is different. Quickest healing is around 1 week, some take several weeks, and 1 month is still normal for a tattoo to fully heal.
  7. Do not rub or pick! As the tattoo heals, it may scab or flake slightly, and will be itchy — DO NOT SCRATCH IT! If you really need to touch your tattoo, please clean your hands first.
  8. Watch for signs of infection: excessive redness spreading beyond the tattoo, swelling, pus/discharge, fever, or increasing pain after the first few days. If you notice these signs, seek medical attention immediately and inform your artist.
  9. A touch-up may be required. After your tattoo is completely healed, if you think parts have dropped out, please contact your artist directly. Since everyone is different, some people may peel less, some peel more.
  10. For any concerns during healing or questions about touch-ups, contact Tridatu Tattoo directly via Instagram — please don't seek treatment advice from unrelated sources.
